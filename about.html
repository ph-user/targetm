COMPANY HISTORY

Foundation & Vision

Focus Media was founded in Shanghai, China, pioneering the concept of elevator advertising and indoor digital media networks.

2003

NASDAQ Listing

Became the first Chinese advertising company to go public on NASDAQ, marking a significant milestone in international expansion.

2005

NASDAQ 100 Index

Included in the prestigious NASDAQ 100 Index, recognizing our growth and market leadership position.

2007

Return to China A-Share

Successfully returned to the China A-share market, strengthening our position in the domestic market.

2015

Collaboration with Alibaba

Alibaba invested 15B RMB for 10% of shares of Focus Media in 2018

2018

Global Expansion

Accelerated international expansion across Asia-Pacific, establishing presence in major metropolitan markets.

2019

Australia Market Entry

Entered the Australia market with operations in Melbourne and Sydney, continuing our strategic global expansion.

2025

MEDIA NETWORK

Focus Media Group's media network spans approximately 300 major cities across China, Korea, Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia, Vietnam, Japan, the United States, and other key markets.
