Company History

Focus Media Group

2003

Focus Media Group was founded, pioneering the concept of elevator advertising media.

2005

Became the first Chinese advertising company to go public on NASDAQ, marking a significant milestone in international expansion.

2007

Included in the prestigious NASDAQ 100 Index, recognizing our growth and market leadership position.
